What

Deforestation has become a critical issue facing our planet, with devastating consequences for both the environment and local communities.

Where

Deforestation occurs globally, but is particularly prevalent in tropical regions such as the Amazon rainforest and Southeast Asia.

How

Deforestation happens through various human activities, including logging, agriculture (e.g. clearing land for crops or livestock), urbanization, and infrastructure development.

About GLYTH

GLYTH is a company dedicated to finding solutions to combat deforestation through the development of interactive simulations and research data. Using cutting-edge technology and a deep understanding of the challenges facing the world's forests, GLYTH aims to create tools and resources that help stakeholders better understand and address the root causes of deforestation.
